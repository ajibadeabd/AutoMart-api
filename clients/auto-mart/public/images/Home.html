Efficiency and productivity

INVXT is a social company focused on the development of rural agricultural communities, with a view to implementing integrated development strategies to serve and support the generation of wealth, driving the development and adoption of technological and research packages.

Mission

To be the reference on the creation and development of sustainable communities, based on agriculture.

Eyesight

To be a national and international reference for quality and innovation in guaranteeing the development of rural communities that survive from agriculture, optimizing existing resources and channeling them into a more competitive perspective.

Values

Competence, innovation, transparency, dedication, loyalty and motivation.

Our services

1

Intermediation

We work with the intermediation of inspected and certified seeds and agrochemicals with origin, marketed within the standards established in the country.

2

Logistics

We offer extensive support, following the entire negotiation process until the final delivery of the product, ensuring peace of mind and customer satisfaction.

3

Training

We promote training in agribusiness value chains, implementation of technologies for profitability, incubation of micro-companies.

INVXT Agro and Food Security

To assist and guide
seed production and facilitate various agricultural inputs for the local community to
increase agricultural production, ensure food security and
make it sustainable.

INVXT Water

Enable the local community to implement and
undertake its own water supply strategy and provide irrigation to
ensure better production.

INVXT Environment

Guarantee the conservation of the environment by
creating a climate adaptation program, enabling women to produce and
replicate native plants to protect the forest, and make it
profitable.

INVXT Energy

Implement a transition strategy for renewable
 energy sources , new technologies to increase agricultural production
making it sustainable, providing irrigation, storage and
processing systems.

INVXT Business

Create a modern and efficient line of
investments in agriculture for local producers and create an
effective pricing system and guarantee access to local and international markets.

IEBA

The Agricultural Based Business Incubator - IEBA is a program developed by INVXT Agro Ivestimentos, which assists micro and small companies, whose main characteristic is to offer products and services on the market with a significant degree of innovation, especially in the field of agriculture and value chains.
